Let’s Get Started!

Applying for Free and Reduced Price School Meals is easy. With just a few minutes, you can provide us with all the information we need to see if your children qualify.

We’ll walk you through the whole process with this online form. There will be instructions to guide you the whole way, but if you’re ever not sure what to do, you can contact Sonya Smith at the Oakland Unified School District, (510)555-555 or Sonya.Smith@OaklandUnified.gov.

You only need to go through the form once, even if your children attend different schools. If you want, you can also save your progress at any time and pick up where you left off later. While completing the application does not guarantee free or reduced priced meals for your children, it should only take a few minutes, and there’s no risk at all in applying.

Why not give it a try?

Please note that this is only a prototype. Information you enter is not secure and will be visible to contest judges. Please do not enter sensitive information at this time.
